General

Product and deployment

What is Power Island and what is it used for?

Power Island is a compact and mobile energy hub, integrated into a 20- or 40-foot container, capable of generating, storing and distributing energy efficiently and sustainably. It is ideal for electrical loads such as charging stations, temporary facilities or remote sites.

When and why is it best to use Power Island?

It is ideal where grid connection is complex, expensive or slow. Its compact and pre-assembled structure avoids major infrastructure investments and can provide an autonomous power source within a few hours after positioning.

In what situations is it more convenient than a traditional grid connection?

When grid connection involves long waiting times, high costs, substations or dedicated lines. Power Island avoids excavation works and complex permits in many temporary or remote contexts.

Can it fully replace a fixed infrastructure?

Yes. It can provide stable and continuous power in off-grid mode and can be relocated when needed.

What are some practical examples of use?

Construction sites, temporary events, exhibitions, fast charging stations, remote industrial sites, temporary logistics bases and emergency situations.

What are the container dimensions?

Two formats are available: 20 feet, about 6 m x 2.4 m x 2.6 m, and 40 feet, about 12 m x 2.4 m x 2.6 m.

Is Power Island mobile or fixed?

It can be used as a fixed installation or as a mobile system, transportable by truck or ship.

How long does it take to install and start up?

Installation and commissioning usually take just a few hours after arriving on site.

Is a special permit required?

In most cases no building permit is required, but this depends on local regulations.

Which sectors is it best suited for?

Events, construction sites, industrial areas, remote communities, temporary charging stations and projects requiring off-grid energy.

Photovoltaics and storage

Solar generation and batteries

How much energy do the photovoltaic panels produce?

Power Island integrates photovoltaic panels on the roof of the container, producing clean energy during daylight hours. Product sheets indicate configurations up to 8kWp or 18kWp depending on format.

Is solar power enough to supply charging stations?

Not directly in every condition: solar production charges the storage system, which supplies charging stations together with the inverter and generator.

What type of batteries does the BESS use?

High-efficiency lithium-ion batteries designed for rapid charge and discharge cycles and long service life.

How long is the battery lifespan?

Under normal conditions, more than 10 years or over 4,000 full charge/discharge cycles.

How long does it take to recharge the batteries?

It depends on source and state of charge. Solar can take several hours, while generator or grid charging reduces time significantly.

Can storage capacity be increased?

Yes, Power Island is scalable and can be configured with additional battery modules.

Generator, grid, AI and charging

Hybrid operation and monitoring

What is an HVO generator?

HVO is Hydrotreated Vegetable Oil, a biofuel derived from vegetable oils and waste fats that can reduce CO2 emissions compared to traditional diesel.

When does the 100 kVA generator start operating?

It activates when batteries fall below a minimum charge level or when load peaks exceed inverter capacity.

Can Power Island operate connected to the grid?

Yes. It can operate independently off-grid or in parallel with an existing power grid, drawing from it or feeding surplus energy back where enabled.

Can Power Island draw energy from the grid?

Yes. When needed, Power Island can use the grid as an additional power source to recharge batteries or support loads during peak demand.

Can Power Island feed energy back into the grid?

Yes. Surplus energy generated by the photovoltaic system or unused by loads can be injected into the grid where the local setup and regulations allow it.

What are the advantages of operating in parallel with the grid?

Hybrid mode maximizes renewable self-consumption, supports service continuity and reduces grid withdrawals during peak times. The integrated software decides when to use internal energy, grid energy or feed excess power back.

How does AI support the system?

AI analyzes historical data and weather forecasts to optimize solar power, batteries and generator use, minimizing costs and waste.

How does the management software work?

The software continuously monitors generation, storage and consumption, making real-time decisions on which source to use for maximum efficiency.

Can I monitor the system remotely?

Yes, remote access via web or dedicated app allows performance, alerts and real-time statistics to be viewed.

Is it compatible with existing systems?

Yes, Power Island can be integrated with photovoltaic systems, generators or other existing energy sources.

How many charging stations can I connect?

The basic system can power up to two 25 kW charging stations simultaneously, with customized configurations available.

Are the charging stations compatible with all electric vehicles?

Yes, the charging stations support common standards such as CCS, CHAdeMO and Type 2.

Is the output power always 25 kW?

The actual power can vary depending on available energy and priority settings.

Is Power Island protected and maintainable?

Yes, it includes electrical protections, temperature sensors and fire-prevention systems. Annual checks of batteries, inverter and generator are recommended.

Can it withstand extreme weather conditions?

The container is designed to resist heavy rain, strong winds, snow and high temperatures.

Does it require periodic maintenance?

An annual check of batteries, inverter and generator is recommended to maintain optimal efficiency.

Can it be purchased or rented?

Both options are available depending on customer needs. Average delivery is between 16 and 20 weeks from order confirmation, depending on configuration.

Can I customize the configuration?

Yes. Panels, batteries, inverters and charging stations can be adapted to specific requirements.

Are incentives or subsidies available?

In many countries, incentives are available for renewable energy and electric mobility. Avoni Industrial can support the application process.
